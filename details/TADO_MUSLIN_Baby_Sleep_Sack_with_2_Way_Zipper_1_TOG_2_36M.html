TADO MUSLIN Baby Sleep Sack

A perfect blend of comfort and freedom for your little one, designed to help them sleep safely and soundly through every season.

Soft & Breathable Material

Made from 95% Rayon from Bamboo and 5% Spandex, this sleep sack offers a cloud-like softness while remaining flexible for easy movement. The breathable fabric helps regulate your baby's body temperature, ensuring they stay comfortable no matter the weather.

Ideal for All Seasons

With 1 TOG insulation, the TADO MUSLIN Sleep Sack keeps your baby warm in winter and cool in summer, making it a perfect year-round solution. This adaptability means you can use it from 2 months up to 36 months, supporting healthy sleep habits throughout their growth.

Easy Access & Safe Design

Equipped with a 2-way zipper, this sleep sack makes it simple to dress or undress your baby, especially during night feeds. The deep, narrow neckline minimizes the risk of suffocation, allowing for a safe and secure sleep environment.

Supports Healthy Development

Designed to encourage natural hip development and free movement, the TADO MUSLIN Sleep Sack helps your baby sleep more comfortably and rest better. Its roomy leg area allows for easy kicking and stretching, promoting healthy sleep patterns.

Why Choose TADO MUSLIN?

This stylish, functional sleep sack is built to last, combining the benefits of a wearable blanket and the freedom of a sleep sack. It's an essential addition to your baby's nursery, offering peace of mind with every bedtime for you and your little one.
